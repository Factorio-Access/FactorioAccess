This is a blueprint book, a collection of multiple blueprints.

Getting blueprints into this book is not done through these menus.

To get blueprints into the book, put this book in your inventory and the blueprint in your hand. Then ctrl shift click on its slot.

This menu consists of quick management commands for all blueprints in the book.

Clicking the name of a blueprint sets it as the active blueprint.  The active blueprint is the blueprint that will be built if you build in the world.

You can drag blueprints to reorder them.

In the world, m, comma, and dot can be used to interact with this book without opening this UI.

m and dot cycle through the blueprints in the book, and comma tells you which one is active.
