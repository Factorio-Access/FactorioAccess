This is the crafting menu.  Clicking items here crafts them.

Shift clicking items will craft 5.

Ctrl shift clicking items will craft as many as you can craft.

You do not need to craft ingredients before you can craft their item. For example, clicking on steam engine will craft the pipes and gears needed for it.

To hear information on a recipe, press k.

To get information on how much you have produced of a specific recipe, press u.
